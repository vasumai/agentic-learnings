# Executive Summary: The Rise of Agentic AI Systems in Enterprise Software (2024-2025)

Agentic AI systems are poised to transform enterprise software by automating complex decisions and operations across key business functions.

- **What:** By 2025, half of large enterprises will deploy agentic AI to drive digital transformation, achieving up to 30% in operational cost savings and scaling markets from $2 billion to $15 billion.
- **So What:** This shift requires major investments and strategic partnerships with leading AI providers to capture efficiency and innovation advantages. Concurrently, enterprises face urgent needs to establish governance frameworks to ensure compliance, manage risks, and maintain stakeholder trust amidst evolving regulations.
- **Now What:** Businesses must prioritize embedding agentic AI into finance, supply chain, and customer service workflows while building robust oversight mechanisms to mitigate liability and security risks stemming from autonomous AI decisions.

**Recommendation:** Act decisively—invest in agentic AI technologies now and form partnerships with specialized vendors to accelerate adoption. Simultaneously, implement comprehensive governance models to balance rapid innovation with risk management, ensuring sustainable competitive advantage and compliance in this new AI-driven era.
